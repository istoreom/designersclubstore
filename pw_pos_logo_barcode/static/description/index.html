Logo & Barcode on POS Receipt

This module is allow to set Logo & Barcode on POS Receipt, Company Logo on POS Receipt and Screen, Pos Barcode Receipt



App Features
  • Company Logo and address on pos receipt
  • Barcode(order number) on pos receipt
Logo and Barcode on pos receipt

You need to activate Logo and Barcode on pos config and use company logo is selected by default to set company logo on pos screen and receipt


In POS receipt you can see company logo, address and barcode


Also you can use other logo for this pos
